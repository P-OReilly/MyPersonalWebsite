Irish Software Developer Based in the Republic of Ireland.



I am a passionate developer, just getting to grips with what the whole thing is all about. I enjoy learning about new technologies, and I love the feeling of seeing results when I finally implement what I have been learning. I have had a passion for all things tech for as long as I can remember, I am constantly searching for tools that make my life more efficient, or more enjoyable. It is exciting to be able to, now, attempt to create these tools myself.

I like to think of applications as an agumentation to our human condition, and I like to think of systems as a process used to manage the ambiguity of our subjective minds. Being such, an application must be intuitive and unintrusive, and systems must be thorough, fault-tolerant and reliable (Trustworthy).
